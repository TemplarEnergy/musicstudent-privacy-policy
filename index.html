MusicStudent Privacy Policy

We at MusicStudent are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us through the MusicStudent mobile application. You can contact us at info@templarenergy.com

Information Collection and Use

MusicStudent does not collect any personal information from its users. The app does not require registration, login, or any form of data input from the user. All data generated or entered within the app, such as student records or invoice information, is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted or accessed by us.

Data Storage

All data created or managed within MusicStudent, including student records, lesson schedules, and invoice details, is stored locally on the user's device. We do not store any data on external servers or transmit any information over the internet.

Third-Party Services

MusicStudent does not integrate with any third-party services that collect or process user data. We do not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or other services that may track user activity.

Children's Privacy

MusicStudent is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly delete such information from our records.
